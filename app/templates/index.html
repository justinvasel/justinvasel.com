Ph.D. Physics Candidate

Indiana University

For my Ph.D. research, I am preparing the NOvA neutrino detectors for the next galactic core-collapse supernova and exploring their sensitivity to underlying physics for a selection of supernova models and progenitor distances. A major component of this research is separating the faint signal in the presence of a large background, a "needle in a haystack" sort of problem. As a part of this effort, NOvA interfaces with the SuperNova Early Warning System (SNEWS), a global network of neutrino detectors dedicated to alerting global neutrino and astronomical communities to an imminent supernova.
